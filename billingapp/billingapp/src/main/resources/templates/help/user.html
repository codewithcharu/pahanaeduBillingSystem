Getting Started

1
Login to Your Account

Access the system using your username and password provided by your administrator.

How to login:
  • Navigate to the login page
  • Enter your username and password
  • Click the "Login" button
  • You'll be redirected to your dashboard
2
Explore the Dashboard

Familiarize yourself with the main navigation and available features.

Main navigation includes:
  • Home: Return to main dashboard
  • Profile: Manage your personal information
  • Bills: View your billing history
  • Help: Access this help guide
3
Update Your Profile

Complete your profile information to ensure accurate billing and communication.

Tip: Keep your contact information up-to-date for important notifications.

Profile Management

Managing Your Profile

Your profile contains important information that affects your billing and account management.

Profile Information:
  • Username: Your unique login identifier (cannot be changed)
  • Full Name: Your complete name as it appears on bills
  • Email: Contact email for notifications and support
  • Phone: Contact number for urgent communications
How to Update Your Profile:
  1. Click on "Profile" in the main navigation
  2. Click "Edit Profile" button
  3. Update the desired fields
  4. Click "Save Changes" to confirm
Important: Your username and user ID cannot be changed. These are unique identifiers for your account.

Billing Features

Understanding Your Bills

As a user, you can view and manage your personal billing information.

What You Can Do:
  • View Bills: See all your billing history
  • Bill Details: Examine individual bill items and totals
  • Print Bills: Generate printable versions of your bills
  • Download PDFs: Save bills as PDF files
Accessing Your Bills:
  1. Navigate to the "Bills" section
  2. View your complete billing history
  3. Click on any bill to see detailed information
  4. Use action buttons to print or download
Tip: You can only view bills associated with your account. This ensures privacy and security.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Check your username and password
  • Ensure Caps Lock is off
  • Contact admin if locked out

  • Ensure you're logged in
  • Check if bills exist for your account
  • Contact admin for assistance

  • Ensure all required fields are filled
  • Check email format is valid
  • Try refreshing the page
